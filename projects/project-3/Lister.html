Project Brief
Lister is a to-do app that lets you add new tasks according to the priority.
The app can help the user in completing all the tasks without getting unnoticed and regretting later about it.
Research
First of all, I needed to know about all the problems related to the previous to-do apps and how a user use that in a general manner. For this, I created a survey that included both qualitative and quantitative questions. The questions set included queries like:
• What is your age?
• Do you use a to-do app?
• How many tasks do you generally save?
• Were you able to complete all the tasks?
• What is the reason that you cannot complete the task even after saving?

The responses I got were like:
"Less Time.
"Forgot to open the app and check.
"Conditions changed since I decided to do the task and it became less
important or I did not get enough time to complete it.
User Persona
Primary
Amber, a 19 year old engineering student who make a list of all the tasks she has to do/ had in mind for 1 week. She sometimes forgot to complete the tasks and get tasks mixed for most of the time.
Secondary
James is a 30 year old employee at an IT company who uses a generic to-do app to save the tasks he has to complete for the day plus also saves the daily household tasks. He uses a to-do app atleast 5-6 times a day.
Pain Points
•Don't have enough time.
•Do not remember tasks.
•Tasks became less important.
•Outdated UI.
